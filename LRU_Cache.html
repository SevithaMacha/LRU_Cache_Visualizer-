How LRU Cache Works

LRU Principle

Evicts the least recently accessed item when cache reaches capacity, keeping frequently used data readily available.

Adding Items

New items go to MRU position. If cache is full, LRU item is evicted to make space.

Accessing Items

Accessed items move to MRU position, updating their priority in the cache.

Visual Indicators

MRU (green), LRU (red), and evicting (orange) items are color-coded for easy identification.
